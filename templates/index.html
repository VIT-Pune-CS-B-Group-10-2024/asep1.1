Introduction to our Project

Our platform offers a powerful tool for simulating and visualizing space radiation effects on materials used in space exploration. Leveraging MATLAB, Python, and D3.js, users can explore how different materials respond to various radiation types in space environments. With an easy-to-use interface, you can customize simulations based on parameters like temperature, radiation type, and material samples. This system helps researchers and engineers optimize spacecraft design, radiation shielding, and material selection for space missions. Explore how our simulations can advance space technology and support safer, more efficient exploration.

About us

We are a team of engineers, researchers, and developers dedicated to advancing space technology through innovative simulations. Our mission is to provide tools that help understand the complex effects of space radiation on materials used in space exploration. By combining expertise in computational modeling and cutting-edge technologies like MATLAB, Python, and D3.js, we aim to support safer and more efficient space missions.
